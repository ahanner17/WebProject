Plastic Pollution

Discover all there is to know about plastic pollution and how it affects the world.

Introduction

The world as we know it is surrounded by plastic. It is singlehandedly the most used man made material and makes our lives so much easier, but plastic has a dark side and it is much darker than you may think.

To first understand any of the topics talked about in this website you must first understand the scale. Do you actually know how much plastic litters the ocean? Hover here to find out.

What to Expect

Throughout this website you will be able to discover all you never knew about plastic. From its history to its dangers, navigate through all the pages to learn about this material and how it affects the world as we know it.
